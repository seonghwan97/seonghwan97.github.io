Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision and machine learning, with a focus on human–object interaction, interactive 3D perception, and hardware–algorithm co-design. Specifically, I aim to develop robust models that integrate learning algorithms with hardware optimization, enabling advanced applications in robotics and intelligent interactive systems.

  • Developing visual recognition and perception models for dynamic human–object interactions.
  • Advancing interactive 3D perception for embodied AI and XR applications.
  • Designing hardware–algorithm co-optimization strategies to achieve efficient, real-time perception.

Ultimately, I aspire to leverage these approaches to enable dynamic, intelligent, and responsive interactions in complex real-world environments.
