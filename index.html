Hi everyone! I’m an M.S. candidate specializing in computer vision & machine learning. I build robust visual models (PyTorch / TensorFlow / OpenCV) that solve real-world problems in logistics, bioengineering, and human–computer interaction.

My recent work includes object detection with YOLOv5 + Transformers (+7.5 pp accuracy), real-time microfluidic mixing control via imaging, and MRI-guided cranial-implant monitoring. I also explore multi-view 3D pose estimation and transformer-based energy-expenditure prediction for XR safety training, plus drone 3D surface scanning for aircraft inspection.
