My name is Seonghwan Lim. I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science at the Illinois Institute of Technology (IIT). I hold a Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Information Engineering from Seoul National University of Science and Technology.

Research Interests

My primary research interests lie in computer vision and machine learning, with a particular focus on developing visual perception systems that interact intelligently with the real world. My current projects include:

  • Analyzing road surface conditions for robust visual recognition tasks (e.g., classifying dry, wet, and flooded conditions; differentiating asphalt and concrete surfaces; detecting cracks and assessing smoothness).
  • Extracting 3D human poses in XR environments to estimate the energy expenditure associated with various human activities.
  • Capturing and quantifying mixing behaviors within microfluidic devices, developing predictive models to precisely control fluid mixtures.

Ultimately, I aspire to leverage advanced visual perception to enable dynamic and responsive interactions in complex real-world scenarios.
